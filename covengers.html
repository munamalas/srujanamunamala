    Hackathon Project. Tools used: Sketch

    ABOUT

    This project focuses on arming doctors with the proper information about COVID-19.  There is a specific form that the doctor/patient can fill out.  There are two options: the doctor/patient can upload their medical history via pdf or they could manually enter the information.  The information provided will help doctors make sure that they have the proper information to make medical decisions.  Lack of information means lack of proper treatment.  This is what Covengers hopes to fix. When I got the opportunity to work on this project, the team had already made a submission.  They just wanted someone to improve the aesthetic and the interface.
